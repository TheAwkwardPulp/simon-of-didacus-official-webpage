The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild Interactive Map

Provided by the popular Zelda wikipedia "Zelda Dungeon", this map offers a variety of useful data points to aid the casual player in their quest to hunt monsters, chests, shrines, quests, and more. Every data point is hyperlinked to a guide on how to complete it, whether that be how best to kill a Lynel or figuring out a shrine's puzzle. Zelda Dungeon's Interactive Map is a reliable tool for any Breath of the Wild player. (Please Note: Some data points are exclusive to the Master Mode DLC but may not be listed as such)
